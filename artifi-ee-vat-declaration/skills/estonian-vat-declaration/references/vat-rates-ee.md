# Estonian VAT Rates

## Current Rates (Effective from July 1, 2025)

| Rate | Application | Effective Since |
|---|---|---|
| **24%** | Standard rate — most goods and services | July 1, 2025 (increased from 22%) |
| **13%** | Reduced rate — accommodation, hotel services, press publications | January 1, 2025 (increased from 9%) |
| **9%** | Reduced rate — books, periodicals, medicines, medical equipment | Long-standing |
| **0%** | Exports outside EU, intra-community B2B supplies | Long-standing |

No VAT rate changes are planned for 2026. The 24% standard rate is permanent.

## Rate History

| Period | Standard | Reduced 1 | Reduced 2 |
|---|---|---|---|
| From Jul 2025 | 24% | 13% | 9% |
| Jan-Jun 2025 | 22% | 13% | 9% |
| Jan 2024 - Dec 2024 | 22% | 9% | 5% |
| Before 2024 | 20% | 9% | 5% |

**Important for transition periods**: When processing invoices near rate change dates,
verify the tax point (supply date) determines the applicable rate, not the invoice date.

## Exempt Supplies (No VAT)

The following are exempt from VAT (no input VAT recovery):
- Financial and insurance services
- Health and social services
- Education services
- Postal services (universal service)
- Certain real estate transactions (with option to tax)

## Important Thresholds

| Threshold | Requirement |
|---|---|
| **EUR 40,000** | Annual VAT registration threshold for businesses |
| **EUR 1,000** | Partner reporting threshold for KMD INF annex |
| **EUR 10,000** | Distance selling threshold to other EU states (before OSS applies) |
