# Management Report (Tegevusaruanne)

Required for all companies except micro-entities.

## Required Content

1. **Overview of business activities** — description of main activities during the year
2. **Key financial indicators and ratios** — with calculation methodology
3. **Significant events during the year** — major contracts, expansions, etc.
4. **Events after balance sheet date** — material post-period events
5. **Expected developments** — outlook for next financial year
6. **R&D activities** — if applicable
7. **Environmental and social responsibility** — for large companies

## Key Financial Indicators

Calculate from ERP data:

| Indicator | Estonian | Formula | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Revenue growth | Muugitulu kasv | (CY Revenue - PY Revenue) / PY Revenue x 100% | Income statement |
| Net profit margin | Puhaskasum marginaal | Net Profit / Revenue x 100% | Income statement |
| Current ratio | Luhiajaline maksevoimekordaja | Current Assets / Current Liabilities | Balance sheet |
| Quick ratio | Kiire maksevoimekordaja | (Current Assets - Inventories) / Current Liabilities | Balance sheet |
| Debt-to-equity | Volakoormuse kordaja | Total Liabilities / Total Equity | Balance sheet |
| Return on equity | Omakapitali tootlus | Net Profit / Average Total Equity x 100% | BS + IS |
| Return on assets | Varade tootlus | Net Profit / Average Total Assets x 100% | BS + IS |
| Personnel expense ratio | Toojoukulude osakaal | Personnel Expenses / Revenue x 100% | Income statement |

For average equity/assets, use: (Opening balance + Closing balance) / 2
