# Fill Gaps in Bookkeeping

Guide me through finding and filling gaps in my accounting:

1. Ask which legal entity and fiscal year
2. Check for payments that don't have matching bills or invoices
3. Show me the gap list: which payments need source documents
4. For each gap, ask if I have the original bill/invoice
5. If yes: read the document and post the bill or invoice
6. If no: help me create a simplified entry based on the payment details
7. Ask about special transactions: loans, investments, fixed assets, intercompany
8. For each special case, guide me through providing the needed documents
9. Handle year-end adjustments: accruals, prepayments, depreciation, provisions
10. Show summary of everything that was posted
